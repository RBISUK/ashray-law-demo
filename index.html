How the band is chosen

  • Late protection: usually **1×–2×** depending on delay/behavior.
  • No prescribed info: often **1×–2×**.
  • Not protected: commonly **2×–3×** (facts matter).

FAQs

        
What is “prescribed information”?
The scheme/landlord must provide specific written information about your deposit and scheme within 30 days of receiving the deposit.

        
Can I claim if I’ve moved out?
Yes, claims can be brought after the tenancy ends. Limitation periods apply — we’ll check dates with you.

        
Will I have to go to court?
Many cases settle before a hearing. If not, we guide you through every step.
